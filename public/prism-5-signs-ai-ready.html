5 Signs Your Business
Is Ready for AI

Is your organization prepared to adopt AI?

1

Your Data Is Organized & Accessible

You have consistent, regularly updated data in accessible formats. The foundation AI needs to deliver real results.

2

You Have Repeatable Manual Processes

Tasks that feel inefficient and repetitive are prime candidates for AI automation. If you can describe the steps, AI can learn them.

3

Your Team Shows Curiosity, Not Fear

When your people see AI as a capability amplifier rather than a threat, you have the cultural foundation for successful adoption.

4

You Have Unanswered Business Questions

If you know what you need to learn but can't easily get the answers from existing data, AI can bridge that gap.

5

You've Identified a Specific Use Case

A bounded scope with a measurable outcome is the best starting point. Start small, prove value, then scale.
