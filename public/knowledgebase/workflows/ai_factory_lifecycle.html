Front door

Public AI Readiness Assessment (no login) — the universal intake. Folder + warm email are automatic; the assessment ask is human-led.

Portal trigger

Any payment opens the client portal (deposit / partial / Net-30 first payment), signalled by the Active Client+ stage — or a manual override.

Six dimensions

Data Infrastructure · Technology Stack · Process Maturity · Team Readiness · Governance & Compliance · Strategic Alignment.

Governing rule

No email auto-fires to a client. Automation is invisible scaffolding; a person leads every touchpoint.

Express lane (deals under $2,500): discovery → one-page proposal + pay link → 100% upfront → work. No MSA/SOW; the assessment is often skipped. Retainers loop at Retainer/Upsell rather than completing at Delivered.

4 · Reference

Readiness bands (what "ready" means) and how each pipeline stage drives automation.

Readiness bands

BandOverallMeaningTypical first move
Emerging< 2.3Foundation not in place — fix data/process firstAI Starter Kit / Data Health Audit
Developing2.3 – 3.2Partial foundation — pilot + paid debriefAI Readiness Assessment (paid) / one pilot
Ready3.3 – 4.2AI-Ready — can run a real project nowAI Workflow Automation, sized to band
Advanced≥ 4.3ScalingFractional AI Advisor retainer
Two foundation gates override the average: data_infra < 2.5 → fix data first; governance < 2.5 → surface a compliance gap.
